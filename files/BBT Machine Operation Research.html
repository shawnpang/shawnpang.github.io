1.2 Imports to Canada

1.2.1 Reistration and Application

  • Business Number: Before importing commercial goods into Canada, as a business or an individual, you will need to obtain a Business Number (BN) issued by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) for an import/export account.
  • Electrical Product Approval: No person shall use, advertise, display, sell, offer for sale or other disposal any electrical equipment unless it has been approved in accordance with Rule 2-024 and Ontario Regulation 438/07.
  • Licensed Customs Broker: We are free to prepare own own release and accounting documentation and transacting business directly with the Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA) or we can authorize a Licensed Customs Broker to act as our agent to transact business.
  • Memorandum D17-1-5 for Registration, Accounting and Payment for Commercial Goods
